Plan International Bangladesh hired 4c Communications as a consultant to conduct training to selected School volunteers and Field Officers (FOs) on Theater for Development (TfD) to improve their capacity to conduct community level Theatre for Development (TfD) show, writing scripts and trained adolescent club members on TfD. The activity designed to cover the out of school students through the TfD issues. I was a Theatre for development trainer in this project. I conducted one day long Theatre for Development training among ESDO officers. Moreover, i was responsible for developing final reports, client communication and assist to develop the TfD manual in this project.
